Careers

Join the Leader in Agricultural Chemistry

At Subito AG Chem, we are at the forefront of agricultural chemistry, specializing in the development and manufacturing of insecticides, pesticides, bactericides, fungicides, plant growth regulators, crop protection, and crop nutrition solutions. Our commitment to innovation and excellence drives our success in enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability.



Why Work with Us?

Cutting-Edge Innovation: Be part of a team that leads the industry in agricultural chemistry. We invest in state-of-the-art technologies and research to develop solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern agriculture. Collaborative Environment: Our success is built on teamwork. We foster a collaborative culture where your ideas are valued and your contributions are integral to our collective achievements. Professional Development: We are dedicated to your growth. From specialized training programs to leadership development, we provide the tools and opportunities for you to advance in your career. Impactful Work: Contribute to projects that have a real-world impact on global agriculture. Our products support farmers in maximizing yields, ensuring crop health, and promoting sustainable practices.

Career Opportunities in Agricultural Chemistry

Explore diverse roles within our company, including: Formulation Chemists: Develop and refine formulations for insecticides, pesticides, fungicides, and plant growth regulators, ensuring efficacy and safety. Agricultural Scientists: Conduct research to improve crop protection strategies, optimize plant nutrition, and advance sustainable farming practices. Quality Assurance Specialists: Oversee the quality control processes for our products, ensuring they meet the highest standards of safety and performance. Production Managers: Lead manufacturing operations, manage teams, and ensure the efficient and safe production of our agricultural chemicals. Regulatory Affairs Experts: Navigate regulatory requirements and ensure compliance for the approval and marketing of our products in various markets.

What we’re looking for

We seek passionate individuals with a strong background in agricultural chemistry and a dedication to innovation. Whether you’re an experienced professional or an emerging talent, if you have a commitment to excellence and a drive to make a difference in agriculture, we invite you to explore opportunities with us.

How to Apply

Ready to make an impact? Browse our current job openings and apply online. Our recruitment team is excited to connect with individuals who are eager to contribute to our mission of advancing agricultural science and supporting global food security.

Shape the Future of Agriculture with Us

At Subito AG Chem, we are more than a chemical manufacturer - we are a partner in agricultural advancement. If you’re looking for a career that combines innovation, collaboration, and real-world impact, we encourage you to explore career opportunities with us today.
