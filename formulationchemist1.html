Formulation Chemist I

Overview

As a Formulation Chemist I, you will develop and optimize formulations for various products, ensuring they meet quality, safety, and performance standards. You will work with raw materials, conduct experiments, and analyze results to support product development and improvement.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and refine product formulations.
  • Conduct laboratory experiments and analyze data.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support product development.
  • Document and report experimental results and observations.
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory and safety standards.
